RESTAURANT RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM

About Our Project



Restaurant Recommendation System based on Collaborative Filtering is a web-based restaurant recommendation system. The primary aim of the application is to suggest a user the best possible choice of food to eat in a given location based on their food targets.

  • We implement a user-based collaborative filtering method to recommend a restaurant personally, based on ratings given by other users.
  • We also use other algorithm such as K-NN algorithm, to predict similar results

It thus offers a chance for people to experience new options, based on their previous likes and choices
